Torna Fort

Torna Fort, also known as Prachandagad, is located in the Pune district and stands at 1,403 meters. As the first fort captured by Shivaji Maharaj at age 16 in 1646, Torna marks the dawn of Maratha sovereignty.

The fort dominated the surrounding region with its strategic height and strong ramparts. It served as Shivaji's first military achievement and inspired the Maratha spirit of independence.

Architectural Features

Torna Fort features massive stone walls, gates like the Maha Darwaja, stunning water tanks, temples, and panoramic watchtowers. Its paths lead through jag generated landscapes and plateaus, showcasing its top-tier defense.
