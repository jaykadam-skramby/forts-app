Shivneri Fort

Shivneri Fort is located in Junnar, Pune district, and is most famous as the birthplace of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in 1630. It is a monument of immense cultural and historic pride.

The fort is known for its strategic layout, fortified walls, and the exact spot where Shivaji was born, which is now marked and preserved for visitors.

Historical Significance

Architectural Features

Shivneri includes solid stone architecture, the Shivai Devi temple, steep pathways, and the famous birth chamber. The fort is well-planned and fortified with bastions and tanks.
