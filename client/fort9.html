Panhala Fort

Panhala Fort, located in Kolhapur district, is one of the largest and most important forts of the Maratha Empire. It served as a strategic base and safe haven for Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.

The fort is best known for the legendary escape of Shivaji Maharaj from Siddi Johar's siege in 1660, using a clever strategy and brave decoy sacrifice by Baji Prabhu Deshpande at Pavan Khind.

Architectural Features

The fort includes massive fortifications, ramparts, the Andhar Bavdi (hidden well), Teen Darwaza, and Amberkhana (granary). Its size and structure show defensive brilliance.

Accessing Panhala Fort

Panhala is 20 km from Kolhapur city and can be reached by road. The fort is motorable, making it suitable for all age groups. It also has a small town around it.

Visiting Hours

Open all days from 6:00 AM to 6:30 PM. Ideal during monsoon and winter for scenic beauty and cool weather.
